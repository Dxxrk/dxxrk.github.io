? What is Ranked Score? ▾

Ranked Score is a single 0–1000 rating that blends every meaningful per-10-minute stat into one number. It's built like an Elo system: an average competitive player scores around 500, and each stat follows an S-curve, so climbing higher always helps, but the gains taper off the further you get from the pack. There's no hard cap, just a ceiling that's tough to reach.

It's designed to be role-neutral: damage and healing are pooled together, so a support's healing counts just as much as a DPS's damage.
